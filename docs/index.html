ABOUT US

Welcome to GM Bakeries, your trusted neighborhood bakery in Handwara town, district Kupwara, conveniently located near Mawar Crossing Road, Chotipora. Since 2003, we have been passionate about crafting delicious, freshly baked goods—whether it’s our irresistible custom cakes, delicate pastries, or hand-crafted artisan bread, everything is made with the finest ingredients and a touch of love. Rooted in family traditions passed down through generations, we’re more than just a bakery. We’re also your one-stop destination for all wedding essentials, including disposable items and supplies, ensuring your big day is memorable and stress-free. With our commitment to fast and friendly service, we proudly offer delivery within a 3 km radius, getting your order to you in just 45 minutes. Founded by Gh Mohd Sheikh, at GM Bakeries, we don’t just bake—we create happiness, one bite at a time!
